Hidden article body

This local fixture simulates a bad paywall implementation where a large amount of article text is shipped in the HTML and only hidden with client-side markup. Site owners should avoid sending gated article bodies to unauthenticated clients. Instead, render a preview and keep the full body server-side until access is authorized. This audit command reports counts and risk signals without printing the hidden body text itself. The purpose is defensive testing for builders, publishers, and developers who want to avoid accidental content leakage.
